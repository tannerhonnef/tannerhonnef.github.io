Geomorphological Effects on Macroinvertebrate Populations

Using Survey123 for data collection and R for data analysis


Team Members:

Frances Atmore and Tanner Honnef

Affiliation:

SFS Chile Directed Research

Project Description:

This project examined the presence and diversity of macroinvertebrate populations in different substrates. Streams were sampled for macroinvertebrates and particle sizes within Southern Patagonia. These results were analyzed and presented to our peers and the community.
